## Libraries / frameworks
- **Gymnasium** — Farama Foundation. RL environment API (successor to OpenAI Gym).
  Repo: github.com/Farama-Foundation/Gymnasium
- **Stable-Baselines3** — Raffin, A., Hill, A., Gleave, A., Kanervisto, A.,
  Ernestus, M., & Dormann, N. (2021). *Stable-Baselines3: Reliable Reinforcement
  Learning Implementations.* Journal of Machine Learning Research, 22(268), 1–8.
- **Arcade Learning Environment (ALE)** —
  Bellemare, M. G., Naddaf, Y., Veness, J., & Bowling, M. (2013). *The Arcade
  Learning Environment: An Evaluation Platform for General Agents.* JAIR, 47,
  253–279. arXiv:1207.4708.
  Machado, M. C., et al. (2018). *Revisiting the Arcade Learning Environment:
  Evaluation Protocols and Open Problems for General Agents.* JAIR, 61, 523–562.
  arXiv:1709.06009.
- **ViZDoom** — Kempka, M., Wydmuch, M., Runc, G., Toczek, J., & Jaśkowski, W.
  (2016). *ViZDoom: A Doom-based AI Research Platform for Visual Reinforcement
  Learning.* IEEE CIG. arXiv:1605.02097. (Gymnasium wrapper: Farama Foundation.)
- **PyTorch** — Paszke, A., et al. (2019). *PyTorch: An Imperative Style,
  High-Performance Deep Learning Library.* NeurIPS 32.
